Our Story

In 1969, Dr. William Laur and Dr. Randal E. Posey founded High Plains Dermatology Center with one goal in mind: improving the quality of life of their patients. Their hope was to show patients how to maintain a healthier lifestyle overall—starting with skincare.

Today, we’ve continued that goal by maintaining a positive, friendly office experience for everyone who enters our practice. We file all insurance paperwork for you and are providers for most networks. We even offer same-day appointments when available.

We’re more than a healthcare provider; we’re a group of highly-trained, experienced professionals working together to give you the best possible care. We are people you know, people you trust, who get the results you want. We always look forward to serving your family’s healthcare needs.

Meet the Doctors

Scott D. Miller, MD, FAAD, FASDS

Board Certified, American Board of Dermatology

Dr. Miller is a board-certified Diplomate of the American Board of Dermatology, a Fellow of the American Academy of Dermatology, and a Fellow of the American Society for Dermatologic Surgery. Born in Alabama, Dr. Miller attended the University of Michigan for undergraduate, received his Doctor of Medicine from Michigan State University, served his internship and residency at Brooke Army Medical Center here in Texas, and is a combat veteran, having also served in the Army as a flight surgeon.

Jason K. Jones, M.D.

Board Certified, American Board of Dermatology

Originally from the San Antonio area, Dr. Jones received his undergraduate degree from the University of Texas. He then went to medical school at Texas Tech University and completed his residency at the University of Texas at Galveston. He joined the clinic in 2014.

Christi A. Baker, M.D.

Board Certified, American Board of Dermatology

Having grown up here in Amarillo, Dr. Baker went on to study medicine at Tulane University in Louisiana, where she received her medical degree and also completed her residency. She has returned home to the Panhandle to practice dermatology, having joined the clinic in 2015.
